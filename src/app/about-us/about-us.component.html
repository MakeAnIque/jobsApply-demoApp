What Are We Doing?

We are trying to solve hiring related problems faced by startups in India. Two most time consuming activities for startup leadership team are a. Funding b. Hiring. Even funding is periodic exercise but hiring is daily time consuming exercise.

Through the help Artificial Intelligence (especially NLP), Blockchain & Voice Chat Bots, we are going to automating entire selection process so that startup founders can focus on their core business areas. Stay tuned for upcoming exciting features!
